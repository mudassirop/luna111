FAQs

How do I place an order?

To place an order, follow these steps:...

1.Click on "Explore the Collections" on the home Page.

2.Search any electronic item that you want to buy then click on order via telegram.

3.After clicking the order button you will come to my telegram where you can give your order with your name and your address.

4.That's it you have successfully placed your order.

for now we accept only Gpay but in future we include more transaction method for our customers.
